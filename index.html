The Story

They Said He Would Never Amount to Anything.

They were wrong. Completely. Totally. Irreversibly wrong.

INDOMITABLE is the true story of Coach Jamie King — a kid from Stafford County, Virginia who overcame learning difficulties, an absent father, crushing self-doubt and circumstances that should have ended the story before it ever began. Instead he built a National Championship coaching career, a broadcasting presence on CBS Radio and ESPN Radio, and a life that has touched thousands.

This book is for every parent, every athlete, every teacher, every veteran, every person still fighting. This is your book. And the best is still ahead.

"They were wrong about you. And this book is going to prove it."

— INDOMITABLE, Chapter One

The Author

Coach Jamie King

Coach Jamie King is the Head Coach of the 1996 AFA National Champion Fredericksburg Generals — a perfect 13-0 season. He is a CBS Radio and ESPN Radio broadcaster, motivational speaker and founder of MVP TAP alongside Hall of Fame partners Mike Singletary, Austin Eggleston and Colin Hatzmann.

His championship story is the subject of Game Ball The Movie — currently in development with Mike Singletary as Producer. He lives just outside of Tampa, Florida.
